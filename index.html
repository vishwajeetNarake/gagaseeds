Forage Seeds

Forage seeds are primarily used by dairy farmers and farmers producing forage for animal feed. These seeds are produced according to the animal species to be fed.

Vegetable Seeds

Hybrid seed is produced by cross-pollinated plants. Hybrid seed production is predominant in modern agriculture for better disease resistance and yield.

Legume Seeds

Legumes are fruit of plants in the pea family. Most legumes are dehiscent fruits that release their seeds by splitting open along two seams.
